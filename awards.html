Trivia

Who had never heard of Twilight before being cast in the film?
Robert Pattinson, who only signed off after learning Kristen Stewart was on the cast. Henry Cavill was author Stephanie Meyer's first choice for the role.
How expensive was Bella's dress for the prom scene?
Only $20, while Edward's suit for the scene was the most expensive costume item of the entire film!
Who didn't know how to drive before filming Twilight?
Robert Pattinson, whose character Edward does quite a bit of driving in the film! In the sequel Breaking Dawn Part 1, he was meant to learn to drive a speedboat - but crashed it while filming.
What was used as a special effect in Edward and James' climactic fight scene?
When Edward bites James' neck, the "flesh" that he tears away is actually swiss cheese! They began using chicken for the flesh, but ran out and resorted to layers of cheese.
What musician wrote a song for the film?
Hayley Williams of Paramore, who is a real life fan of the books. Robert Pattinson's songs are used on the soundtrack as well!
How much did Forbes estimate Carlisle Cullen's fortune to be worth?
$34 billion - and he was ranked #1 on their list of richest fictional characters of 2010, surpassing even Scrooge McDuck!
What surprising Nickelodeon child star auditioned for the role of Edward?
Josh Peck of Drake and Josh fame - who actually made it to the final four list of actors considered for the role!
What line did Robert Pattinson hand pick for his character Edward?
"Hold on tight, spider monkey" - other contenders for the scene were "Prepare for liftoff" and "Got a good grip?"
